The Chef Behind the Magic

A Passion Forged
in Kitchens Worldwide

Born in kenya to a family that loved passing on the cooking traits down to us as children, I loved cooking since i was 15. My culinary journey spiked at 20 years old i opened a small eatery , did fast food deliveries, I proceeded to Paris France studied fine dining culinary and worked in 3 different style restaurants. Every experience was unique creating a good environment to thrive and grow skill fully,

Today i do private home services, and do meal preps and cater for small events
